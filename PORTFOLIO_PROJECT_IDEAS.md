## ðŸ“ˆ Tips for Building Your Portfolio

### 1. **Quality Over Quantity**
- 3-5 polished projects beat 10 half-finished ones
- Each project should be fully functional and bug-free

### 2. **Show Real Problem-Solving**
- Include case studies explaining your process
- Document challenges you faced and how you solved them

### 3. **Include Live Demos**
- Deploy your projects on Vercel, Netlify, or Railway
- Ensure demos are always accessible

### 4. **Write Clean Code**
- Use proper folder structure and naming conventions
- Include comprehensive README files
- Add comments for complex logic

### 5. **Diversify Your Tech Stack**
- Show proficiency across frontend, backend, and databases
- Include at least one AI/ML project (currently in high demand)

### 6. **Target Specific Industries**
- Build projects for industries you want to work in
- Research their common pain points

### 7. **Keep Projects Updated**
- Fix bugs and add features periodically
- Update dependencies and security patches
